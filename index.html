About Me

I’m Focused on Creating Meaningful Experiences Through Design.

Interested to work as a UX/UI Designer, to help conduct and participate in user research, task planning and enhance the functionality and usability of platforms by developing user persona and scenario for the target audience. Develops wireframes, site maps, User Flow , prototyping and creates designs based on client specifications and documents user experience. Collaborates closely with the app and web development team to bring prototypes into the production code process. Implements user-centered/ focused designs and processes in order to identify specific user needs and find the best solutions.
